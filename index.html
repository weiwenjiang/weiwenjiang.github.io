Short Bio:
Dr. Weiwen Jiang is currently a Post-Doctoral Research Associate at the University of Notre Dame. He received Ph.D. from Chongqing University in 2019. From 2017 to 2019, he was a research scholar in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Pittsburgh. His reserach works have won two Best Paper Awrads in NVMSA’15 and ICCD’17, and four Best Paper Nominations in ASP-DAC’16, DAC’19, CODES+ISSS’19 and ASP-DAC’20. In his first postdoc year, he received research funds from Edgecortix Inc. via NSF I/UCRC as the principal investigator (PI). [Details]
Research Interests:
Embedded Systems, FPGAs, Parallel Systems, Non-Volatile Memories, Neural Networks, Supply Chain Planning
