Short Bio:
Dr. Weiwen Jiang is currently a Post-Doctoral Research Associate at the University of Notre Dame. He received the Ph.D. degree from Chongqing University in 2019. From 2017 to 2019, he was a research scholar in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Pittsburgh. His research works have won two Best Paper Awards in NVMSA’15 and ICCD’17, and four Best Paper Nominations in ASP-DAC’16, DAC’19, CODES+ISSS’19 and ASP-DAC’20. In his first postdoc year, he received research funds from NSF IIS and Edgecortix Inc. [Details]
